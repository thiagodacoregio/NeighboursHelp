How It Works

Getting started with NeighboursHelp is simple and straightforward

1

Sign Up

Create your profile and join your neighborhood community. It's free and takes just a few minutes.

2

Post or Browse

Share what you need help with or browse requests from neighbors. From groceries to pet sitting, we've got you covered.

3

Connect

Get in touch with neighbors through our secure messaging system and arrange to help each other.

4

Build Community

Create lasting relationships and a stronger neighborhood through mutual support and friendship.
