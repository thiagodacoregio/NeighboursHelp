Frequently Asked Questions

Is NeighboursHelp free to use?

Yes! NeighboursHelp is completely free for all community members.

How do I verify my neighbors?

We use address verification and community ratings to ensure safety and trust.

What if I need help urgently?

Mark your request as "urgent" and nearby neighbors will be notified immediately.

Can I help neighbors in other areas?

Currently, we focus on local neighborhoods, but you can join multiple area groups.
